We approach every project by creating effective and innovative marketing solutions while honoring the heritage that inspires and unites our community.

Our Founder

Having been engrossed in the luxury retail space since 2019, Aashna Sharma has become an innovator in beauty. Starting at the age of 15, she began her own color cosmetics business called Shared Planet. At Shared Planet, she developed multiple successful influencer campaigns featuring Nicole Guerriero and Christen Dominique. Through this work she rapidly developed her own network of creators and professionals within the retail industry. While simultaneously growing her own indie brand, she worked for NARS Cosmetics and Oribe Hair Care, understanding how to communicate the values of heritage brands while staying on trend in a competitive landscape. Within these roles she has established brand partnerships with Roblox and NYFW.

Inspired by the work she has done and the amazing fellow entrepreneurs she has met along the way, in 2024, Aashna launched BINDAAS. Aiming to communicate the visions of other South Asian businesses and amplify the beauty of South Asian culture, she looks forward to every new challenge.

Her work across her company and various others, she has received features in British Vogue, Glossy, and Beauty Independent and had her products carried across BoxyCharm and Verishop. She currently resides in Brooklyn, NY with her adorable corgi, Queso.
